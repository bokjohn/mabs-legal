BreathEasy Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 12, 2026
Effective Date: March 12, 2026

Overview

BreathEasy ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information through the BreathEasy mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

  • Breathing Exercises: Exercise preferences, duration, frequency, and completion data
  • Wellness Notes: Optional personal notes about your mood or anxiety levels
  • App Settings: Your preferences for notifications, sounds, and language
  • In-App Purchases: Transaction information related to your subscription purchases

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifier
  • App Usage: Features accessed, exercises used, frequency of sessions
  • Diagnostics: Crash reports and performance metrics

Information NOT Collected

  • Health Data: We do NOT collect heart rate, blood pressure, or biometric data
  • Location: We do not access your location
  • Camera/Photos: We do not access camera or photo library
  • Contacts: We do not access your contact list

2. Data Collection Philosophy

BreathEasy does NOT collect health information. We are an educational wellness app, not a medical device. Your breathing session history is optional and stored entirely on your device—we don't upload it to our servers.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve BreathEasy services
  • Process in-app purchases and manage subscriptions
  • Deliver guided breathing content
  • Send optional reminders (if you opt-in)
  • Diagnose and fix technical issues
  • Improve user experience through aggregate analytics

4. Local Data Storage

All personal wellness data is stored locally on your device. We do NOT transmit your breathing history, notes, or session data to our servers. You have complete control over your data.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do NOT sell, share, or license your personal data. Your wellness information remains private.

We may share information only:

  • With service providers assisting with app operation
  • As required by law or to protect safety
  • With your explicit consent

6. Data Retention

Wellness data is retained on your device until you delete it. When you uninstall BreathEasy or delete records, data is erased locally. Diagnostic logs are retained for up to 90 days.

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Local data encryption
  • Secure transmission of any data sent to servers
  • Regular security updates

8. HIPAA & Medical Compliance

BreathEasy is NOT a medical device and does NOT provide medical advice. If you have anxiety, panic disorder, or other health conditions, please consult a healthcare professional. Our app is supplemental only.

9. Third-Party Services

BreathEasy may use:

  • Firebase Analytics: For crash reporting and performance monitoring
  • Apple/Google Services: For in-app purchase processing
  • Content Delivery: For audio streaming of guided sessions

These services have their own privacy policies.

10. Children's Privacy

BreathEasy is appropriate for ages 13+. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If a child has provided information, contact us immediately.

11. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Withdraw consent at any time

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use constitutes acceptance of changes.
