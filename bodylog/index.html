Privacy Policy — BodyLog

Last Updated: March 9, 2026

What Data Do We Collect?

BodyLog stores your body measurements locally on your device.

Data stored locally:

  • Body measurements you log
  • Weight, dimensions, photos
  • Dates and notes
  • Progress tracking

Data NOT collected:

  • We don't collect personal information
  • We don't send data to servers
  • We don't track health conditions
  • We don't have user accounts

Important: Not Medical Device

BodyLog is a personal tracking tool, not a medical device. For health concerns, consult a healthcare provider.

How Your Data Is Protected

Your measurement data is stored securely on your device. Only you can access it.

Your Rights

  • Access: All your data is on your device
  • Delete: Uninstall BodyLog, data is deleted
  • Privacy: We don't track you
