ScreenFree Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 12, 2026
Effective Date: March 12, 2026

Overview

ScreenFree ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information through the ScreenFree mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

  • Screen Time Goals: Your target daily screen time limits and preferences
  • App Tracking: Which apps you choose to monitor
  • Usage Data: Time spent in tracked apps (optional tracking)
  • Settings: Notification preferences, theme selection
  • In-App Purchases: Transaction information for subscriptions

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, OS version, device identifier
  • App Usage Metrics: Features used, tracking frequency
  • Diagnostics: Crash reports and performance data

Information NOT Collected

  • Content Access: We do NOT read messages, emails, or app content
  • Location: We do not access location data
  • Camera/Microphone: We do not access audio or video devices
  • Passwords: We do not access or store passwords

2. How We Use Your Information

We use information to:

  • Help you monitor and manage screen time
  • Provide personalized insights and recommendations
  • Send optional reminders and notifications
  • Improve app features and performance
  • Process in-app purchase transactions
  • Diagnose technical issues

3. Local Data Storage

All screen time tracking data is stored locally on your device. We do NOT upload your app usage history to our servers. You maintain complete control over your data.

You may optionally back up data through iCloud/Google Drive using your own account. We do not access these backups.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do NOT sell your data. We do NOT share screen time analytics with app developers or third parties.

We may share information only:

  • With service providers assisting with app operation
  • As required by law
  • To protect user safety
  • With your explicit consent

5. Data Retention

Screen time data is stored until you delete it. Uninstalling ScreenFree removes all local data from your device. Diagnostic logs are retained for up to 90 days.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encrypted local storage
  • Secure data transmission
  • Regular security updates
  • No public cloud backups of personal data

7. Third-Party Services

ScreenFree may use:

  • Firebase Analytics: For crash reporting and app performance
  • Apple/Google Services: For in-app purchases and device functionality

These services have their own privacy policies.

8. Parent/Guardian Controls

ScreenFree includes features for parents to monitor their children's screen usage. If you use ScreenFree to monitor a minor:

  • Inform the minor that monitoring is active
  • Comply with local laws regarding parental monitoring
  • Respect the minor's privacy rights

9. Children's Privacy

ScreenFree is designed for ages 13+. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If a child has provided information, contact us immediately.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request deletion of tracking data
  • Opt-out of analytics
  • Withdraw consent anytime

Contact bokjohnkim@gmail.com to exercise these rights.

We may update this Privacy Policy. Continued use constitutes acceptance of updates.
