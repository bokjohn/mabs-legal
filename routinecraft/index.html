Privacy Policy — RoutineCraft

Last Updated: March 9, 2026

What Data Do We Collect?

RoutineCraft stores your routines and habits locally on your device.

Data stored locally:

  • Routines you create
  • Habit tracking (completion data)
  • Notes and reminders
  • Custom schedules

Data NOT collected:

  • We don't collect personal information
  • We don't track outside the app
  • We don't send data to servers
  • We don't have user accounts

How Your Data Is Protected

Your routine data is stored securely on your device (iOS encryption). Only you can access it.

Your Rights

  • Access: All your data is on your device
  • Delete: Uninstall RoutineCraft, data is deleted
  • Privacy: We don't track you
