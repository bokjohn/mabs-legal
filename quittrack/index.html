Privacy Policy — QuitTrack

Last Updated: March 9, 2026

What Data Do We Collect?

QuitTrack tracks YOUR quit journey locally on your device.

Data stored locally:

  • Quit start date
  • Progress tracking (days quit)
  • Notes and milestones
  • Any personal goals you add

Data NOT collected:

  • We don't collect personal information
  • We don't track your health (medical data)
  • We don't send data to servers
  • We don't have user accounts

Important: Not Medical Advice

QuitTrack is a tracking tool, not medical advice. If you need health support, consult a healthcare provider.

How Your Data Is Protected

Your quit-tracking data is stored locally on your device in encrypted storage. Only you can access it.

Your Rights

  • Access: All your data is on your device
  • Delete: Uninstall QuitTrack, data is deleted
  • Privacy: We don't track you
