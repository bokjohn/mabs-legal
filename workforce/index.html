Privacy Policy — Workforce Management

Last Updated: March 11, 2026

What Data Do We Collect?

Workforce Management stores your employee scheduling and workforce data locally on your device.

Data stored locally:

  • Employee names and contact information
  • Shift schedules and assignments
  • Time tracking and work hours
  • Notes and scheduling preferences
  • Company-specific scheduling rules

Data NOT collected:

  • We don't collect personal information beyond what you enter
  • We don't track employee location or behavior
  • We don't send data to external servers
  • We don't have user accounts or cloud sync

How Your Data Is Protected

Your workforce data is stored securely on your device. Only authorized users with access to your device can view this information. iOS encryption protects all data at rest.

Employee Privacy

As a manager using Workforce Management, you are responsible for:

  • Obtaining proper consent from employees before storing their data in this app
  • Complying with employment laws in your jurisdiction regarding data retention and access
  • Protecting employee privacy by restricting access to scheduling data
  • Notifying employees about how their schedule information is stored and used

Your Rights

  • Access: All your workforce data is on your device
  • Delete: Uninstall Workforce Management, data is deleted
  • Privacy: We don't track you or your employees
