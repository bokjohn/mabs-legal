SleepScore Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 12, 2026
Effective Date: March 12, 2026

Overview

SleepScore ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information through the SleepScore mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

  • Sleep Logs: Sleep duration, bedtime, wake time, and quality ratings
  • Sleep Patterns: Notes about sleep quality, factors affecting sleep
  • App Settings: Preferences for reminders, notifications, language
  • In-App Purchases: Subscription payment information

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, OS version, device identifier
  • App Usage: Features accessed, frequency of log entries
  • Diagnostics: Crash reports and performance metrics

Information NOT Collected

  • Biometric Data: We do NOT collect heart rate, breathing patterns, or motion data
  • Location: We do not access your location
  • Camera/Microphone: We do not access audio or video devices
  • Contacts: We do not access your contact list

2. Data Collection Philosophy

SleepScore does NOT collect medical data. We are a sleep tracking app for personal awareness, not a medical diagnostic tool. All sleep logs are stored on your device—we do NOT upload them to our servers.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use information to:

  • Help you track and understand sleep patterns
  • Provide personalized sleep insights
  • Send optional sleep reminders and notifications
  • Improve app features
  • Process in-app purchases
  • Diagnose technical issues

4. Local Data Storage

All sleep data is stored locally on your device. We do NOT transmit your sleep logs, notes, or personal patterns to our servers. You maintain complete control and ownership of your data.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do NOT sell your sleep data. We do NOT share your sleep patterns with healthcare providers, researchers, or third parties.

We may share information only:

  • With service providers assisting with app operation
  • As required by law
  • To protect safety
  • With your explicit written consent

6. Data Retention

Sleep data is retained on your device until you delete it. Uninstalling SleepScore removes all local data. Diagnostic logs are retained for 90 days.

7. Security

We implement security measures:

  • Local data encryption
  • Secure transmission
  • Regular updates
  • No centralized cloud backups

8. Medical Disclaimer

IMPORTANT: SleepScore is NOT a medical device. It does NOT diagnose sleep disorders or provide medical advice.

If you experience:

  • Chronic insomnia
  • Sleep apnea symptoms
  • Persistent fatigue
  • Other sleep disorders

Consult a healthcare professional or sleep specialist.

SleepScore is for personal awareness only and should not replace medical evaluation.

9. Third-Party Services

SleepScore may use:

  • Firebase Analytics: For crash reporting and performance monitoring
  • Apple/Google Services: For in-app purchases

These services have their own privacy policies.

10. Children's Privacy

SleepScore is designed for ages 13+. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Contact us immediately if a child has provided information.

  • Opt-out of analytics
  • Withdraw consent anytime
