Privacy Policy — EverSince

Last Updated: March 9, 2026

What Data Do We Collect?

EverSince stores your relationship timeline locally on your device only.

Data stored locally:

  • Relationship start date
  • Notes you add
  • Photos you upload
  • Memories you create

Data NOT collected:

  • We don't collect your personal information
  • We don't track how you use the app
  • We don't send data to servers
  • We don't have user accounts

In-App Purchase

  • EverSince uses RevenueCat for premium features ($2.99/month)
  • RevenueCat handles payment data (Apple/card info), not us
  • See RevenueCat's privacy policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

How Your Data Is Protected

Your relationship data is stored in Apple's secure local storage (encrypted by iOS). Only you can access it.

Your Rights

  • Access: All your data is on your device (not ours)
  • Delete: Uninstall EverSince, data is deleted
  • No tracking: We don't track you
