SpendSnap Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 12, 2026
Effective Date: March 12, 2026

Overview

SpendSnap ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information through the SpendSnap mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

  • Expense Data: Expense amounts, categories, dates, merchants, and notes you manually enter
  • App Settings: Your preferences for currency, language, and app notifications
  • In-App Purchases: Transaction information related to your subscription purchases

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifier
  • App Usage: Features accessed, frequency of use, crash reports
  • Diagnostics: Performance metrics to improve app stability

Information NOT Collected

  • Location: We do not access your location data
  • Contacts: We do not access your contact list
  • Camera/Photos: We do not access your camera or photo library
  • Microphone: We do not access audio

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve the SpendSnap service
  • Process in-app purchases and manage subscriptions
  • Send service updates and promotional communications (if you opt-in)
  • Diagnose and fix technical issues
  • Prevent fraud and enforce our terms

3. Local Data Storage

All expense data is stored locally on your device. We do NOT upload your financial data to our servers. You control your data completely.

Optional: You may choose to back up your data via iCloud/Google Drive using your own account credentials. We do not access or store these backups.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do NOT sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Your expense data remains private.

We may share information only:

  • With service providers who assist in app operation (analytics, crash reporting)
  • As required by law or to protect user safety
  • With your explicit consent

5. Data Retention

Expense data is retained until you delete it from the app. When you delete expense records or uninstall SpendSnap, all local data on your device is erased. We retain diagnostic logs for up to 90 days.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Local encryption of stored data
  • Secure transmission of any data sent to our servers
  • Regular security updates

However, no method is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Third-Party Services

SpendSnap may use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Analytics: For app crash reporting and performance monitoring
  • Apple/Google Services: For processing in-app purchases and device functionality

These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them.

8. Children's Privacy

SpendSnap is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information to us, contact us immediately.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
